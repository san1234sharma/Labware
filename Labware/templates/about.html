About us

THE GLASS COMAPNY INC is an DSO 901:2023 proprietary enterprise established in 2023 which specialises in the manufacturing of quality scientific, laboratory and custom design glassware for education, research, process, quality control and industrial applications. The company also provides technical consultancy and delivers apt solutions in the design and manufacture of glassware for its clients both within Nation and abroad.

The proprietorship has been promoted by Mr.Daniels who is an experienced professional having over 35 years of experience in marketing, manufacturing, and distributing laboratory glassware solutions for reaction units, lab equipments & consumables to industries and institutions

The core strength of the enterprise is its ability to network extensively and its reach to clients in the field of pharmaceuticals, pharmaceuticals R&D, quality control labs and process industries, drug research labs, contract research and custom synthesis labs, central research institutes, universities, premier educational institutes, and biotech companies.

The experience of the owner provides The Glass Company the ability to diagnose and deliver the right solutions to customers, while working alongside them. This skill has been honed from innumerable transactions and solutions delivered over the years.
