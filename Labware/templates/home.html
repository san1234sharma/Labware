About us

THE GLASS COMAPNY INC is an DSO 901:2023 proprietary enterprise established in 2023which specialises in the manufacturing of quality scientific, laboratory and custom design glassware for education, research, process, quality control and industrial applications. The company also provides technical consultancy and delivers apt solutions in the design and manufacture of glassware for its clients both within Nation and abroad.

Services

Our services are included in various fields

Borosilicate and quartz scientific glassblowing

Borosilicate and quartz are primary materials used in scientific glassblowing due to their exceptional properties, allowing for the creation of durable, heat-resistant glassware vital for laboratory settings, catering to different experimental needs, and providing thermal shock resistance and chemical inertness.

Custom Design glass fabrication

Custom design glass fabrication entails crafting unique glass products tailored to specific client needs, employing various techniques such as glassblowing, cutting, etching, and assembly for bespoke creations.

Prototype and development projects

Prototype and development projects involve the creation and refinement of initial models or versions of products, systems, or technology to test and enhance their functionality, design, and feasibility before full-scale production or implementation.

Graduating, grinding, and cutting

Graduating, grinding, and cutting are crucial processes in the realm of glasswork, involving the meticulous shaping, smoothing, and resizing of glass materials to achieve specific dimensions, shapes, and finishes for diverse applications in industries such as manufacturing, art, and scientific instrumentation.
